Power Supply Module

This module simulates a single-channel bench power supply and exposes message-driven control commands through the protocol. It is intended to support sequence examples that configure setpoints, enable output, and read live telemetry.

Supported API commands: configure_output (voltage, current_limit), enable_output (enabled), set_load (load_resistance), and read_output (status response). The simulation tracks setpoints and computes measured voltage, current, and power with current-limit behavior.
