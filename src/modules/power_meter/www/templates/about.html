Power Meter Module

This module simulates a power meter that serves voltage, current, and power measurements for sequence-driven tests. It is designed to operate through protocol messages and remain decoupled from direct module-to-module method calls.

Supported API commands: measure (channel), set_noise (noise_percent), and read_status (current settings and last measurement). The simulation samples the power_supply readback and applies configurable noise to emulate realistic measurement variation.
